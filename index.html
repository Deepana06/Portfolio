    • Customer segmentation

    Programming Language:

  • Python

    • Description:

  • Focused on customer segmentation using Python. This involves applying several analytical techniques to better understand customer behaviour and preferences. Two key methods used were RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) and the K-means clustering technique. RFM analysis is a method commonly used in marketing and retail to segment customers based on three main criteria: Recency: How recently a customer made a purchase or interacted with the business. Frequency: How often a customer makes purchases or engages with the business over a specific period. Monetary: How much money a customer spends on purchases or transactions within a given timeframe. By analysing these factors, RFM analysis helps categorize customers into different segments such as high-value customers, loyal customers, potential churners, etc. In addition to RFM analysis, employed the K-means clustering technique. In the context of customer segmentation, K-means clustering helps identify groups of customers with similar characteristics or purchasing behaviours.
